Hue Sense

A quiz for the colors whose names you know but whose coordinates you don't — vermilion, chartreuse, cerulean. Each round locks the one axis the name gives away (azure is obviously blue) and asks you to place the other two on the square.

Tap where you think the color sits. The third HSB axis is fixed for you — baked into the gradient. You'll be scored by how close you land, then shown the true spot.
